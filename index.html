Who We Are

A family business built on trust since 2000

Maltia 2000 - Trade & Marketing Ltd. is a family-owned Israeli company established in 2000. The company initially specialized in the sale of building materials and sanitary products for residential and commercial projects.

In 2010, the company expanded its operations by opening a paid parking facility, which has since become its primary business activity. We remain committed to providing reliable service, professionalism, and long-term customer satisfaction.
